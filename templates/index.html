Welcome to The IEEE Student Branch Chapter, University of Birmingham

This is the Home Page of the IEEE UoB Student Branch Chapter website! Our website is continually updated with all of the latest lectures and events that the Branch is running – check back often to make sure that you don’t miss out!!!
IEEE Student Branch Chapter at The University of Birmingham was inugrated in February 2020 at the University. It started as the IEEE UK and Ireland PES Chapter. We are based within the School of Engineering, University of Birmingham. We also hold the reporting duties of the IEEE Student Branch at UoB in addition to out IEEE PES activities.
